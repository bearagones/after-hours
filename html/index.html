After Hours

Why After Hours


Since the pandemic, San Francisco has seen earlier closing times, leaving fewer options for late-night dining and socializing. After Hours was created to help night owls find spots that stay open beyond the typical closing hours—whether you're looking for a place to grab a meal, enjoy a drink, or spend time with friends. With this guide, you can explore SF’s nightlife without worrying about closing times.
